It started without intention. I left the house because staying inside felt heavier than the cold.

![one](https://64.media.tumblr.com/c226f10ed9a7cb497945eece6a75a6d1/567c37d23c29cb6c-2e/s500x750/d99c6d7580cb6d5801eb455f7f3b4eb4f688b8c6.jpg)

The streets were empty, but not quiet.

![two](https://64.media.tumblr.com/c226f10ed9a7cb497945eece6a75a6d1/567c37d23c29cb6c-2e/s500x750/d99c6d7580cb6d5801eb455f7f3b4eb4f688b8c6.jpg)

A man waited for a bus that never came. Or maybe he was waiting for something else.

I kept walking.
